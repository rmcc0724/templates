What's changing for 2019.

Prior Authorization
If you have UnitedHealthcare, you may be required to get prior authorization for complex health services like surgery and clinical trials. For questions, call UnitedHealthcare at 1-866-348-1286.

Life and Accident Insurance Programs
Premiums are increasing for life and AD&D insurance for you and your spouse or domestic partner. Take time to evaluate how much coverage is right for you. It may be that Apple already provides all the coverage you need. 2019 premiums are available in the benefits Enrollment Tool.

Medical Insurance Programs
Medical premiums are increasing for most plans due to the rising costs of treatment and advanced technologies. For all plans, the increase is no more than 6$ per paycheck. 2019 premiums are available in the benefits Enrollment Tool.
